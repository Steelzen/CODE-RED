The registration of the application will be done in different part, here is the part for the user

The HTML page will have a form with different text and date input

The user will acces a form where he could register, for the registration he will need to fill:

First and last name, his date of birth, Email address, signature, password and the password confirmation

There is some restriction on the password, he will need to be at least 8 characters long, with on capital letter and one special character

All those informations will past with a Post to the back end where a function will add all the information to the database
Also, in the back end we will assure that the account isn't already here, if he is nothing will happen
After all those instruction, the account is not finalize, an administrator need to validate it
